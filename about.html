About CODE4KIDS

CODEZ4KIDS is a program primarily during vulnerable after school and weekend hours for children to learn how to code. The purpose of this organization based program is to keep children intrigued, interested, and busy during vulnerable hours where they may be directed to msichief and boredom. The reason for it all is also simply to pave the way. Children are the future and so is technology, which is why the program was created and is needed.

Meet The CEO *

The founder of this organization has a strong passion for teaching and Technology, hence why she has a degree in both fields. Tasha has always had an interest in creating a real time non-profit organization for minorities in low income neighborhoods and then branching out to offer the program EVERYWHERE!
