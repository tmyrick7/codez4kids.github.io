Introduction to CODEZ4KIDS

This organization was created to introduce coding programs to kids who have excitement with video games, computers, STEM, and more. The program was created by the CODE4KIDS organization to heighten the amount of children we have who are interested in the tech field because well, they are the future. Future software developers, future engineers, quality engineers, etc. While many may not be aware of the rise and demand of individuals in the software development and engineering departments, this is a great start to provide parents, schools, and other organizations insight to the importance of technology and coding.
